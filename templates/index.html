Welcome to Drawing Assistant

At Drawing Assistant, we believe that everyone has an artist within them waiting to be discovered. Using our software, you can learn the fundamentals of drawing from your own device. Simply upload the painting you wish to recreate, and our drawing assistant will dissect it and guide you through each step of the drawing process. The techniques we provide are employed by some of the most renowned artists globally. Wishing you the best of luck in your drawing journey!

What materials should I use?

There are many different mediums and ways to color your paintings. For beginers we highly suggest oil pastels and oil paint. For paper and a medium to draw on we suggest canvas mi-tienes. For a pencil set/charcol we suggest Generals. These are just suggestions, go with whatever you feel comforatable with.

About Us

Drawing Assistant was created by a group of middle schoolers in Cumberland, Rhode Island. Our goal is to help people of all ages and skill levels explore drawing in a supportive environment. We use high-tech tools to make drawing easier for you.
