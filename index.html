Drawing Assistant

Welcome to Drawing Assistant

At Drawing Assistant, we believe that everyone has an artist within them waiting to be discovered. Using our software, you can learn the fundamentals of drawing from your own computer. You can upload any painting that you would like to draw, and the drawing assistant breaks down the painting for you showing you step by step how to draw it. The steps that we show to help you draw your painting are used by even the best artists in the world. Good luck drawing!

What materials should I use?

There are many different mediums and ways to color your paintings. For beginers we highly suggest oil pastels and oil paint. For paper and a medium to draw on we suggest canvas mi-tienes. For a pencil set/charcol we suggest Generals. These are just suggestions, go with whatever you feel comforatable with.

About Us

Drawing Assistant was made by a local group in Cumberland, Rhode Island composed of middle schoolers. Our mission is to foster creativity and provide a supportive environment for individuals of all ages and skill levels to explore the world of drawing. We offer high level technology to aid you in your drawing journey.
